About the data

We’re going to look at the oysters in the “Saint Augustine” Region of the oyster monitoring effort and further examine spat abundance with environmental parameters over time. Once again the oyster data was collected by the Guana Tolomato Matanzas National Estuarine Research Reserve as part of an oyster monitoring pilot project led by Head Biologist Pamela Marcum. A report with this information can be found here.

The water quality data comes from the System-Wide Monitoring Program (SWMP) water quality station at the mouth of the San Sebastian River in St. Augustine, Florida within the Guana Tolomato Matanzas National Estuarine Research Reserve. This data was collected using a continuous data sonde recording every 15-minutes from 2015-2020 and was aggregated to monthly values.
